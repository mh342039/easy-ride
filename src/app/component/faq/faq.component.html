Frequently Asked Questions
1. Can I get a refund for my ride if I do not travel?

We recommend reviewing the cancellation and refund policies indicated on your booking confirmation email. In order to qualify for Easy ride refund, in the form of a voucher, you must cancel your ride up to 30 minutes before the scheduled departure time indicated on your ticket. After cancelling you'll receive a voucher (valid for 1 year) for the amount of the booking. Use this voucher to book your next ride.

2. Why didn't I receive a booking confirmation email?

If you haven't received a confirmation email with your ticket information within 10 minutes of making your booking, we recommend checking the different mailboxes available: spam, junk mail, promotions etc.
